Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse is a museum devoted to Claude Monet and the luminous world of Impressionism.

From plein air landscapes to shimmering seascapes, we highlight how Monet captured light, atmosphere, and movement.

Explore our signature Monet collections and seasonal exhibitions in one visit.

Our newest exhibition features late‑period Monet alongside select works from fellow Impressionists, tracing the movement’s evolving approach to light.
