Explore MonoMuse

Mission Statement

MonoMuse is an Impressionist artists museum that celebrates the movement’s pioneers, with Claude Monet featured in our most recent exhibition.

We present rotating shows that highlight distinct voices in Impressionism while connecting their techniques to the light‑filled approach Monet refined.

Recent Exhibition Timeline

  • 2023: Camille Pissarro — rural life, city streets, and atmospheric color.
  • 2021: Berthe Morisot — intimate interiors and luminous portraiture.
  • 2019: Mary Cassatt — modern life, motherhood, and bold composition.
  • 2026: Claude Monet — the latest exhibition on light and water.
